The Journey with Theodora

The Journey with Theodora is a private counselling and wellness practice dedicated to providing compassionate, ethical, and professional psychological support to individuals navigating personal challenges, relationships, and life transitions.

The practice offers a safe, confidential, and non-judgmental environment where individuals can explore their experiences, develop emotional resilience, and work toward personal growth and well-being.

The practice is symbolised through blank line art drawings that represent the counselling journey. These drawings illustrate the connection between the counsellor and the survivor, who work together to gradually bring colour and healing into the artwork. In the same way, the counselling process supports individuals as they move from difficult experiences toward healing, clarity, and a more vibrant life.

About Us

About the Practice

The Journey with Theodora was founded with the purpose of supporting individuals as they navigate life’s complexities, emotional challenges, and important personal decisions.

The practice is grounded in the belief that every individual has the capacity for growth, healing, and transformation when supported in a safe and supportive environment.

Through evidence-informed counselling approaches, the practice assists clients in developing self-awareness, strengthening coping strategies, and building the confidence needed to move forward in their personal journeys.

About the Counsellor

Theodora Masalesa

Wellness Counsellor – ASCHP

Registration Number:WC25/17124

Theodora Masalesa is a Wellness Counsellor with the Association for Supportive Counsellors and Holistic Practitioners (ASCHP). She is passionate about providing psychological support, emotional guidance, and empowerment to individuals and communities.

Her professional journey began during her time as a student when she volunteered in support services assisting individuals experiencing trauma and crisis. During this period, she gained valuable experience in crisis intervention, survivor support, and community-based assistance.

After four months of volunteering, she was appointed as an Intervention Specialist, where she further developed her skills in counselling support, case management, and advocacy for vulnerable individuals.

Theodora holds a Bachelor of Arts in Health Sciences and Social Services from the University of South Africa (UNISA), with majors in Community Psychology and Health Psychology.She has also completed a Personal Growth and Counselling Skills course through Lifeline Johannesburg, further strengthening her professional counselling competencies.

Her work is guided by empathy, professionalism, and a strong commitment to ethical and client-centred care.

Our Services

The practice provides counselling and emotional support services designed to promote personal development, psychological well-being, and healthy relationships.

Therapeutic Approach

The counselling approach at The Journey with Theodora is client-centred, trauma-informed, and strengths-based.

Each counselling process is collaborative and tailored to the unique experiences, needs, and goals of the client. The aim is to create a supportive environment where individuals feel heard, respected, and empowered to explore their experiences and develop meaningful strategies for change.

Through this process, clients are supported in building self-understanding, emotional balance, and the confidence to move forward in their personal and professional lives.

Why Choose The Journey with Theodora

The practice is committed to providing ethical and compassionate care that prioritises the well-being and dignity of every client.

Key values include:

• A safe, confidential, and supportive counselling environment

• Trauma-informed and ethical practice

• Specialised focus on youth career guidance

• Experience supporting individuals facing complex and sensitive challenges

• Compassionate, respectful, and non-judgmental care

Career Guidance for Youth

This service provides support for young individuals navigating academic pressures, career uncertainty, and major life transitions.

Key focus areas include:

• Career exploration and informed decision-making

• Identifying personal strengths, interests, and values

• Building confidence and motivation

• Coping with academic pressure and uncertainty

• Goal setting and future planning

Relationship Counselling

Relationship counselling provides support for individuals experiencing challenges within personal, family, or romantic relationships.

Focus areas include:

• Communication difficulties

• Emotional awareness and self-esteem

• Developing healthy personal boundaries

• Family and peer relationship challenges

• Understanding and improving relationship patterns

Psychological and Emotional Support

The practice provides compassionate support to individuals experiencing emotional distress, trauma, or overwhelming life circumstances.

Areas of support include:

• Gender-based violence

• Sexual abuse and domestic violence

• Trauma and emotional distress

• Personal growth and self-development

• Emotional regulation and coping strategies
